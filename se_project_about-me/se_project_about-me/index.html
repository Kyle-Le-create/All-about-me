Muay thai

I started doing muay thai since my freshman year in college. First, I started doing boxing for footwork and punching control, than I did wrestling to practice grappling. But my boxing coach suggested I should do muay thai and I know so little about this martial art. When I first started it was a lot of conditioning and jump roping. Than I realize it was a lot of kicking and elblows, because muay thai is the art of the eight limbs. I feel in love with the sport and respected my coach for teaching me to be discipline towards my training, school and my life.
